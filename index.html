Have you met Ken?

Ken is the name of this 29-year-old creative and problem-solving handyman from Troms, Norway.
He loves challenging his creativity, whether through web development, 3D modeling, playing musical instruments, or repairing and detailing cars.
With a background in carpentry, music, photography/editing, and web development,
it's safe to say that he enjoys keeping his brain active by tackling tasks where he can solve problems and be creative at the same time.
